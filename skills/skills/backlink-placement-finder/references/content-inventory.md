## How to Match Content to Anchors

The goal is to find the social.plus page that is the **most contextually relevant** to the anchor text found in the partner's article. Prioritize:

1. **Direct topic match** — the social.plus page is literally about the anchor topic (e.g., anchor "app engagement" → blog about app engagement strategies)
2. **Glossary definitions** — for generic/definitional anchors, glossary pages are ideal (e.g., anchor "user engagement" → glossary/user-engagement)
3. **Depth match** — if the anchor is specific (e.g., "app engagement metrics"), prefer the blog post that dives deep into that subtopic over a general overview
